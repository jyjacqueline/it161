About Me

Jacqueline (Jackie) Nguyen is a data analyst skilled in analytics reporting and modeling, data governance, and project management. She has worked in various industries such as technology, video game production, healthcare, higher education, and non-profits. She has a passion for local music industry, diversity in tech, and information systems. Jackie's goal is to create and present data in a way that is easily understood and can help people—especially those of underrepresented groups—make more informed decisions.​
