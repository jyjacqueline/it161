About the client: Midnight Freqs

Client Name Matthew Bindewald
Business Name Midnight Freqs, LLC
Redesign Redesign of the staff biography page
Website Type Entertainment, Informational
Client Goal The current bio page for Midnight Freq's staff has not been launched yet as business priorities have been elsewhere. This page is to recognize the valuable people that keep the small business running on the day to day.
Audience Group Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Artist Agent:
  • An artist agent is the bridge between talent and the music industry, securing opportunities that align with their artist's vision and goals.
  • With keen communication and negotiation skills, the artist agent recommends where their artists should play and the overall vision of their performances.
  • The agent needs to negotiate production and venue deals with companies like Midnight Freqs to host their artists' shows.
  • The Midnight Freqs page is useful for the agent to show the artist examples of what kind of music production their show would like.
  • After exploring the showcased work that Midnight Freqs has done, the biography page will direct these agents with questions to the correct correspondence based on the staff member's role listed.
Marketing Manager:
  • The Marketing Manager at the music production company like Midnight Freqs is a skilled strategist and creative force, driving impactful campaigns that publicly showcases the company's stage production and services.
  • With a keen understanding of the industry, they passionately craft innovative marketing strategies to engage audiences and maximize the company's reach.
  • Marketing managers need a platform to send potential clients an overview of the work that their company like Midnight Freqs can provide.
  • In addition to production package prices and etc., directing potential clients to this new staff biography webpage is helpful for marketing managers to showcase the valuable staff responsible for building these amazing events.
Audience Member:
  • Arguably the most important stakeholder in the music business is the crowd. Music enthusiasts bring joy and appreciation of the diverse impact of music on their lives.
  • Their enthusiasm creates a magical atmosphere, inspiring performers and celebrating the profound influence of music within the community.
  • Music enthusiasts not only appreciate the artist, but the overall production of the show that enhances their experience. Many audience members want to send their appreciation or suggestions to the event producers.
  • The Midnight Freqs website not only has resources to join the local music community scene with information on future events, but the biography page will also provide faces of who puts on these amazing events including how to contact them.
